Our Mission

Animal Alliance of Canada (AAC) believes in the protection of all animals and to the promotion of a harmonious relationship among humans, non-humans and the environment. We do this by using electoral politics and lobbying as strategies to make the legislators pass sound animal and environmental protection laws.

AAC is a federally incorporated non-profit organization, founded in August of 1990. We have supporters across Canada and are funded entirely by private donations.

How We Do It

Legislating Change

We engage in electoral politics and lobby for legislation that better protects animals and the environment we share.

Political Action

We created the Animal Protection Party of Canada (AAPC) – a federally registered political party and North America’s first political party focused exclusively on environmental and animal protection - to give related policies political relevance at the municipal, provincial and federal levels of government.

Animal Rescue

We run a network that rescues lost, stray, and abandoned pets. We also help and rehabilitate animals that have been exploited in research labs.
